# Pairs Trading

In essence, pairs trading aims to construct a mean-reverting process by combining price signals that contain drift. If such an endeavour is successful, one has a statistical arbitrage. A long position initiated at a price well below the mean of the process will generate a positive return when the process will reveret to its mean. The opposite is be true for a short position.
